Mitchell D. Adler, P.A.

Mitchell D. Adler has consistently distinguished himself as a skillful, diligent, and client-focused attorney—both in and out of the courtroom. Known for his professionalism, integrity, and compassionate approach, Mitch is dedicated to delivering tailored legal solutions focusing on common-sense approaches that serve each client’s unique needs and objectives.

Recognized for excellence in the legal community, Mitch is among the top 10% of attorneys to receive the highest AV® Preeminent™ rating from Martindale-Hubbell, reflecting exceptional peer-reviewed standards of legal ability and ethical conduct. His practice focuses on business law, commercial litigation, real estate, trust and probate litigation.

Mitch earned his Juris Doctor, cum laude, from New York Law School in 1984 and was admitted to the Florida Bar that same year. He holds a Bachelor of Arts from the State University of New York at Albany, awarded in 1977.

In addition to his legal practice, Mitch has contributed to the legal community through teaching and service. He served as an Adjunct Faculty Member in the Paralegal Studies Program at Broward College (2009–2014), acted as a Certified Arbitrator for the 17th Judicial Circuit Court of Broward County, and was a member—and later Chair—of the Florida Bar Grievance Committee for the 17th Judicial Circuit (Committee “E,” 2001–2003).

Mitch was honored as a Top Lawyer by the South Florida Legal Guide for 14 consecutive years (2003–2016) and holds a “Superb” rating from AVVO, a respected national attorney directory and ratings service.

To learn more about Mitchell D. Adler, P.A., or to schedule a consultation, please visit our Contact section. We look forward to helping you or your client navigate your legal challenges with confidence.
